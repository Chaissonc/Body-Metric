Designer & Developer · Las Vegas, NV

I built Sarx because I couldn't find a tool that gave me everything I needed in one place — without having to already know what I was looking for. I wanted something that met people where they are and walked them forward.

Everything in this app is built with user experience as the first priority. If something feels like work, it gets redesigned until it doesn't.

THE PROBLEM

Most health tools either overwhelm you or tell you nothing useful.

Other calculators throw a single number at you with no context. What does your BMI actually mean? How does your TDEE connect to your goals? What's a caloric deficit, and why does it matter?

Most people leave those tools more confused than when they arrived — or they just stop caring.

WHY SARX EXISTS

Your health, in plain language.

Sarx was built for everyday people who want to finally understand their body — not just see a number, but know what it means and what to do about it.

Every screen is designed to remove friction. No sign-ups to get started. No noise. Just your data, clearly explained, with your next step right in front of you.

THE SCIENCE

Trusted formulas. Real evidence.

Every calculation in Sarx is based on widely accepted, peer-reviewed formulas used by researchers and registered dietitians — including the Mifflin-St Jeor equation for BMR, the Katch-McArdle body fat method, and established activity multipliers for TDEE.

The Learning Library ranks habits and supplements by clinical evidence tier — so you always know what's well-supported versus what's just trending.
